I’m Rani
A hypnotherapist, coach and life strategist; devoted to helping you achieve ultimate fulfilment in every aspect of your life. I’ll share with you my secrets to creating profound changes; and moving past previous limitations.
My Journey

I was born and raised in Sydney. My 6 siblings grew up more than a decade ahead of me. So, I got the INSIDE TRACK in life by learning from the pain and struggles of those around me.

“So, how did you get into this?”I hear you ask.

This unique situation gave me an unparalleled education in life from a very early age. I am forever grateful for the incredibly DIVERSE family I grew up in. I would not be the therapist I am today without them.

I’ve lived most of my adult life outside of Australia in such amazing places as London, Beijing and Jakarta. Living an international life with my husband and two kids is an awesome adventure. We’re now living the ultimate life in Bali, and we LOVE it here. Well, what’s not to love, right?!?!

The one thread that runs through my entire career (and life) is my passion for helping people. Whether it’s been my own businesses, or running a corporate event company in China, I have an outlook that focuses on going above and beyond, to deliver what it is that people really need – even if they don’t even know they need it yet!

“I love inspiring people to move past what ever it is that is holding them back”

After the umpteenth time of being told “you should be a life coach”, I finally started to take the idea seriously. It wasn't just my friends and family telling me this, it was also my colleagues, clients, acquaintances, and even random strangers – all telling me the same thing. I thought, how bizarre that they were all telling me the same thing?!?! Well, duh Rani!!! Helloooooooooo!

Even though I was great at what I was doing, I just didn't have that sense of fulfillment I was looking for. I knew this wasn’t “it” for me.

“You know that feeling when you ‘should’ be happy but you’re just not feeling it?”

On the surface, my job ticked all the boxes: creative autonomy, amazing boss, exciting challenges, fascinating work stories to tell parties etc. etc… I had a nagging sense that there was something bigger for me to do. I was soon to discover, that my ultimate purpose was to help people find true well-being and freedom from the trappings of our own self-imposed limitations.

But like so many of us, I was afraid to make that leap.

And here’s the magic… It was actually a nightmare-client-from-hell that gave me the final push INTO THE UNKNOWN. Have you ever had that feeling when everything suddenly turns to $#!+, and you’re drowning in misery? Well, that was me. Some days were so unbearable that I literally cried when I opened my computer in the morning to emails from said client. The annihilation was real. I couldn’t see the big picture, and despaired at which way to turn.

“Why is this happening to me?” – we’ve all been there, in our own private moments of victimhood.

Eventually, I learned to trust that it was all happening for a reason, and indeed it was.

So, here’s special shout-out to the straw that broke the camel’s back: THANK YOU, because without you, I might still be kickin’ back in my comfort zone. And we ALL know that…

Nothing good EVER comes from staying in your comfort zone.

I then set about turning my passion – helping people be their absolute best – into a successful career. I’ve now developed my own incredibly powerful and unique strategy of to instantly transform the lives of my clients.

What I’ve realized is this…

No matter how fortunate you are in this life, sometimes you may NEED HELP with something.
